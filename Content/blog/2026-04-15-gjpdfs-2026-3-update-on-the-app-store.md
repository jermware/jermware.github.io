# gjPDFs Updated on the App Store

[**gjPDFs**](/projects/gjPDFs) has been updated to **v2026.3** on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/gjpdfs/id6760037275).

This is an enhancement release with the following changes:
- Zoom to 100% now displays the page at its true physical size.
- Zoom to Fit Page now scales the page to fill the available view area — whichever dimension (width or height) is the limiting factor.
