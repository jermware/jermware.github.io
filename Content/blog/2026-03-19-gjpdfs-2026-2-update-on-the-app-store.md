# gjPDFs Updated on the App Store

[**gjPDFs**](/projects/gjPDFs) has been updated to **v2026.2** on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/gjpdfs/id6760037275).

This is an enhancement release with the following changes:
- Added support for dropping image and text files to insert pages into PDF document.
