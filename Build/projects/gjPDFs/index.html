gjPDFs



  A fast, focused PDF editor for macOS.

gjPDFs is a lightweight PDF viewer and editor built for people who need to organise their documents without the bloat of full-featured PDF suites.

View and Navigate Open any PDF and navigate with ease. Use the page thumbnail sidebar to browse visually, or switch to the table of contents view to jump between sections. Search for text with the built-in Find bar, or go directly to any page number.

Edit Your Table of Contents Build and reorganise your PDF’s table of contents from scratch. Add, rename, and delete outline entries. Drag and drop items to reorder them or nest them under other entries to create a clear, hierarchical structure.

Rearrange and Manage Pages Reorder pages by dragging thumbnails to new positions. Delete pages you no longer need. Insert pages from other PDF files — their table of contents entries are automatically merged into yours.

Full Undo and Redo Every change you make — whether it’s moving a page, editing the table of contents, or inserting pages from another file — can be undone and redone. The Edit menu shows exactly what action will be undone or redone so you always know where you stand.

Designed for macOS gjPDFs is a native Mac app built with SwiftUI. It supports keyboard shortcuts throughout, remembers your recent documents, and follows macOS conventions for saving, closing, and quitting.

Features:

  • Thumbnail and table of contents sidebars.
  • Drag-and-drop page reordering.
  • Insert pages from other PDFs with automatic TOC merging.
  • Full undo/redo for all editing operations.
  • Find text with match navigation.
  • Zoom to fit width, fit page, actual size, or custom levels.
  • Keyboard navigation for the table of contents.
  • Go to Page by number.
  • Recent documents menu.
